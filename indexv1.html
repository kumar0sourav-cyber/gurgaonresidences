Masterplan & Location

The epicentre of India’s future of commerce, community and connection

Located at Sector 103 on Dwarka Expressway, the development is positioned for connectivity across Delhi and Gurugram, with nearby access to key business, airport and civic infrastructure corridors.

The masterplan emphasizes life without compromise: zero vehicle movement at ground level, elevated towers on approximately 20 to 30-foot-high stilts, dedicated activity tracks, and panoramic views of landscaped central areas.
